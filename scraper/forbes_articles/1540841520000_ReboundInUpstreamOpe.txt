BP Plc. (NYSE:BP) is set to report its financial numbers for the September quarter on 30th October 2018. The European integrated energy company is expected to post a notable improvement in its revenue as well as earnings due to the rebound in commodity prices. Further, the company has successfully commenced several major upstream projects under budget and on or ahead of schedule, which are likely to contribute to its production growth. In addition, the company’s recent acquisition of BHP’s US onshore assets is expected to provide access to high quality assets in the Permian-Delaware, Eagle Ford, and Haynesville basins. Since these are low cost-high margin unconventional plays, this deal is likely to be accretive to the company’s earnings and cash flow and drive its value in the coming years.
