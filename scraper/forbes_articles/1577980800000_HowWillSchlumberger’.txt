Schlumberger (NYSE: SLB) is an oil-services firm based out of four executive offices in Paris, London, Houston, and The Hague. It competes with other oil-drilling and services companies such as Halliburton and Transocean. The fracking industry has been facing a slowdown recently due to lower oil prices, because of which oil-services companies have seen their revenues slowing down. Despite this slowdown, Schlumberger continues to beat expectations from its earnings releases.
