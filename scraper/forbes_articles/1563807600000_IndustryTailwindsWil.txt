United Airline Holdings is an airline major based out of Chicago, Illinois. It operates 4,900 flights to 355 destinations across five continents. In 2018 it carried over 158 million passengers and flew over 1.7 million flights. The company reported Q2 results last week. During the quarter, United overcame issues stemming from its inoperative 737-MAX fleet to post better-than-expected numbers.
