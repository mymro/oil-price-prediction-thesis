Royal Dutch Shell (NYSE: RDSA) is a multinational oil major based out of The Hague, Netherlands. It employs over 82,000 people and competes with a range of other oil majors including Exxon Mobil, British Petroleum and Chevron. Royal Dutch Shell’s revenues have been under pressure in 2019 due to the decline in oil prices. However, as detailed in our interactive dashboard, we expect Shell’s top line to increase by 3% for 2020 after having shrunk by roughly the same amount over 2019.
