European shares finished mixed on Wednesday, as investors digested a slew of earnings from European companies. The pan-European Euro Stoxx 600 Index closed around the flatline, with most sectors in the red after a choppy session. In the U.S., stocks traded in a narrow range on Wednesday as investors weighed mixed reports from major corporations.
