Asia markets were largely negative on Thursday, on the back of U.S. Federal Reserve Chairman Jerome Powell's comments on inflation after the central bank decided to increase interest rates. In the Greater China region, Hong Kong's Hang Seng index was lower by 0.3 percent as of 3:15 p.m. HK/SIN.
