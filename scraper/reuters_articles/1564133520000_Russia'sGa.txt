MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russia’s Gazprom said on Friday its subsidiaries had completed the sale of Gazprom’s ordinary shares and its free float would rise to 46.02% as a result. The company had said on Thursday that subsidiaries Gazprom Gerosgaz Holdings BV and Rosingaz Ltd would offer a combined stake of 693.6 million ordinary Gazprom shares, or 2.92%.
