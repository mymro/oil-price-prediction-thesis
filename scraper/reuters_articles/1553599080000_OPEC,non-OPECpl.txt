LONDON/DUBAI (Reuters) - OPEC and its allies aim to hold their next ministerial monitoring committee meeting, known as the JMMC, on May 19 in the Saudi Arabian city of Jeddah, two sources said on Tuesday. Saudi Arabia and Russia are members of the JMMC, which includes other major oil producers who took part in a global supply cut agreement last year, including Iraq, the United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, Nigeria and Kazakhstan.
