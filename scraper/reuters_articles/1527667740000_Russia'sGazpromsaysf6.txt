MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian gas giant Gazprom said on Wednesday its first-quarter net income had risen 11 percent to 371.6 billion roubles ($6 billion), thanks to higher gas prices and sales volumes, beating analysts expectations. Analysts polled by Reuters expected Gazprom’s net income to stand at 338.3 billion roubles. Gazprom’s sales in January - March increased by 18 percent to 2.14 trillion roubles.
