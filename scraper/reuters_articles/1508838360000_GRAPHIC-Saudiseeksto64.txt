Oct 24 (Reuters) - Hundreds of bankers, business leaders and government officials from around the world are attending a three-day conference in Riyadh. Arranged by Saudi Arabia’s main sovereign wealth fund, the $180 billion Public Investment Fund (PIF), the event is labelled the Future Investment Initiative and is an effort to present the world’s top oil exporter as a leading global investment destination.
