LONDON, Feb 28 (Reuters) - * Loadings of Forties crude oil slowed down on Wednesday due to bad weather and overnight snowfall, two trading sources familiar with the matter said * Pilots are unable to reach the tankers as they arrive to bring them alongside to load oil * It was not immediately clear if oil flows through the Forties pipeline were affected * A spokesman for Ineos could not be reached for immediate comment * Forties is one of five North Sea crude grades used to set the dated Brent benchmark (Reporting by Julia Payne and Ahmad Ghaddar; editing by Jason Neely)
