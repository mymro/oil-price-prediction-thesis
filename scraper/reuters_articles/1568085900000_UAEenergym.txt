CAIRO - (Reuters) - The United Arab Emirates Energy Minister Suhail Mohamed Al Mazrouei congratulated the new Saudi Arabian energy minister, Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman, in a tweet. Al Mazrouei said he trusts the naming of Prince Abdulaziz to head the energy ministry of the world’s largest oil exporter will strengthen the Saudi kingdom’s leading role in the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC).
