SINGAPORE, Jan 15 (Reuters) - CME Group Inc said on Tuesday it plans to launch an e-auction platform with Enterprise Products Partners LP for U.S. spot crude oil export cargoes to be priced based on West Texas Intermediate (WTI) Houston futures. “The electronic auction will provide a competitive and transparent venue for firms across the world to efficiently hedge U.S. crude oil,” CME said in a statement. The initial focus of the e-auction platform will be on the crude infrastructure terminals in Houston, managed by Enterprise Products Partners, CME added.
