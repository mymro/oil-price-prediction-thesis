MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian lender Sberbank said on Thursday that it and Gazprombank had agreed to provide project financing worth 100 billion rubles ($1.7 billion) to the Vostochno-Messoyakhskoye oil project. The oil project is operated by Russian oil majors Rosneft and Gazprom Neft. Sberbank said in a statement it would provide 60 billion rubles, and that Gazprombank would cover the remainder of the 10-year financing project.
