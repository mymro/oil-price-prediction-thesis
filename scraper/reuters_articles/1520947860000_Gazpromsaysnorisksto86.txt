MOSCOW, March 13 (Reuters) - Russia’s gas row with Ukraine poses no risks to gas supplies to Europe, Russian state news agency TASS quoted Gazprom’s CEO Alexei Miller as saying said on Tuesday. Miller, at a meeting with Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev, also said it will take up to two years to terminate contracts with Ukraine, TASS reported.
