BAGHDAD, March 26 (Reuters) - Iraq supports OPEC’s agreement to cut oil output, Iraqi President Fuad Masum said during a meeting on Monday with OPEC Secretary-general Mohammad Barkindo. Masum, who met Barkindo in Baghdad, stressed the “need to support the deal to cut crude oil supply”, according to the state-run Iraqi Media Network website. (Reporting by Ahmed Rasheed; editing by Jason Neely)
