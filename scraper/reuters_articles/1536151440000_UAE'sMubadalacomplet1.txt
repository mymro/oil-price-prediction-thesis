MOSCOW, Sept 5 (Reuters) - Abu Dhabi’s state-owned Mubadala fund has completed a deal on investment in oil production in Russia’s western Siberia, the Russian Direct Investment Fund, a partner in the deal, said in a statement on Wednesday. Mubadala Petroleum has bought a 44 percent stake in Gazprom Neft-Vostok LLC, the operator of 13 oilfields, from Gazprom Neft . RDIF has acquired 5 percent in the company.
