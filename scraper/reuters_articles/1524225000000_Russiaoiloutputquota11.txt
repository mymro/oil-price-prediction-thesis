MOSCOW, April 20 (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak said on Friday that OPEC and non-OPEC countries could change oil production quotas either in the direction of further cuts or by easing the curbs, TASS news agency reported. Interfax news agency also quoted Novak as saying that “we can’t say today” if a decision on changing output quotas will be taken at the OPEC and non-OPEC meeting in June. (Reporting by Vladimir Soldatkin and Maria Kiselyova; Editing by Susan Fenton)
