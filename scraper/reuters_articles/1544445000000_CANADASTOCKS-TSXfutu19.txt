Dec 10 (Reuters) - Stock futures pointed to a lower opening for Canada’s main stock index on Monday, as crude oil prices fell and in line with lower global equities markets. December futures on the S&P/TSX index were down 0.33 percent at 7:00 a.m. ET. Canada’s housing starts and building permits data is due at 8:30 a.m. ET. The Toronto Stock Exchange’s S&P/TSX fell 141.87 points, or 0.95 percent, to 14,795.13 on Friday. Dow Jones Industrial Average e-mini futures were down 0.27 percent at 7:15 a.m. ET, while S&P 500 e-mini futures were down 0.27 percent and Nasdaq 100 e-mini futures were down 0.22 percent.
