March futures on the S&P TSX index were up 0.01 percent at 7:15 a.m. ET. Canada’s main stock index closed higher on Friday, lifted by strong gains in oil and metals prices. Dow Jones Industrial Average e-mini futures were up 0.37 percent at 7:15 a.m. ET, while S&P 500 e-mini futures were up 0.31 percent and Nasdaq 100 e-mini futures were up 0.56 percent.
