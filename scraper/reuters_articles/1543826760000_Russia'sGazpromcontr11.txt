MOSCOW, Dec 3 (Reuters) - Russia’s Gazprom lent Gazprombank, the country’s third biggest lender, 15.5 billion roubles ($233.5 million) via a subordinated loan in the period between January and September, Gazprom’s quarterly report showed on Monday. Gazprom is a shareholder in Gazprombank. In the same period last year, Gazprom lent 3.9 billion roubles to Gazprombank via a subordinated loan in order to shore up its banking capital.
