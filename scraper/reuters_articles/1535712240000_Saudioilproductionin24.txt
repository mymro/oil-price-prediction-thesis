DUBAI, Aug 31 (Reuters) - Saudi Arabia’s crude oil production in August rose to 10.424 million barrels per day compared to 10.288 million bpd in July, an OPEC source said on Friday. Crude supply in August was 10.467 million bpd, the OPEC source said. That’s compared to 10.380 million bpd in July. Supply to the market - domestically and for export - may differ from production depending on the movement of barrels in and out of storage.
