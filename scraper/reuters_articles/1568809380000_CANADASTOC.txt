Sept 18 (Reuters) - Futures for Canada’s main stock index inched higher on Wednesday ahead of the U.S. Federal Reserve’s monetary policy decision. September futures on the S&P/TSX index were up 0.02% at 7:00 a.m. ET. Canadian inflation data is due at 8:30 a.m. ET. The Toronto Stock Exchange S&P/TSX composite index rose 0.50% to 16,834.75 on Tuesday. Dow Jones Industrial Average e-mini futures were down 0.07% at 7:00 a.m. ET, while S&P 500 e-mini futures were down 0.13% and Nasdaq 100 e-mini futures were down 0.13%. The Minnesota Supreme Court declined on Tuesday to hear environmental and tribal challenges to Enbridge Inc’s Line 3 oil pipeline, a decision that removes one potential obstacle for the already-delayed project.
