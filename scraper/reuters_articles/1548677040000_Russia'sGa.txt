MOSCOW, Jan 28 (Reuters) - Russian gas producer Gazprom said on Monday it had budgeted for a dividend payout of 10.43 roubles per share for 2018, the Interfax news agency reported. The news agency also quoted a source as saying Gazprom could meet with U.S. investors to discuss a dollar bond placement next week.
