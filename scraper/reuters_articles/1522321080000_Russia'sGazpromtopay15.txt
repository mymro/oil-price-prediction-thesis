MINSK (Reuters) - Russia’s gas giant Gazprom will repay 600 billion rubles ($10.40 billion) worth of outstanding loans in 2018, the company’s Chief Financial Officer Andrei Kruglov said on Thursday. Kruglov also said Gazprom has no plans to issue bonds denominated in Japanese yen this year but will consider such an option in 2019. ($1 = 57.6707 rubles)
