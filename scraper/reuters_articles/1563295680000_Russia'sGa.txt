MOSCOW (Reuters) - Russian state gas giant Gazprom’s natural gas exports outside the former Soviet Union declined by 5.6% to 102.8 billion cubic meters (bcm) between Jan. 1 and July 15 compared to a year earlier, the company said on Tuesday. Gazprom’s natural gas output over the same period rose by 2.3% to 276.3 bcm from a year earlier, the company said.
