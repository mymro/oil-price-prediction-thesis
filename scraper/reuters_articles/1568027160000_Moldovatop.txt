CHISINAU, Sept 9 (Reuters) - Moldovan President Igor Dodon said on Monday that the price Moldova pays for Russian gas would fall $10-15 in October from the current level of $235 per thousand cubic metres. Russian gas producer Gazprom may further reduce the price by $40-50 from Jan. 1 and by $70 from April 1, Dodon said at a briefing.
