(Adds company news items and futures) Jan 14 (Reuters) - Britain’s FTSE 100 index is seen opening 32 points lower at 6,886 on Monday, according to financial bookmakers, with FTSE 100 futures down 0.48 percent ahead of the cash market open. PageGroup: Recruitment firm PageGroup Plc reported a 15.4 percent rise in fourth-quarter profit on Monday, driven by strong hiring in Europe, Middle East and Africa. JD SPORTS: JD Sports Fashion Plc said on Monday it expected full-year headline profit at the upper end of market expectations, as Britain’s biggest sportswear retailer did not cut prices for products and maintained its margins. OPHIR: Ophir Energy Plc said on Monday that it has rejected Indonesian oil and gas group Medco Energi Internasional Tbk PT’s potential buyout offer, saying it “undervalues” the British energy company.
